# Svelda Brand Document

## What is SVELDA?
Svelda is a premium brand specializing in high-end, luxury essentials for cats and their owners. It bridges the gap between pet accessories and sophisticated home decor. The product line includes natural beds, beautifully crafted bowls, and elegant toys. 

## The Mission
Svelda's mission is to bring harmony and style to the home by offering meticulously crafted cat essentials made from natural, premium materials. The brand believes that simple, beautiful things make life better and aims to provide a better life, together for both the pet and the owner.

## The Style
The style is distinctly premium, minimalist, and artful. It emphasizes a harmonious blend of form and function. Products are designed to be timeless and integrate seamlessly into any modern or classic interior, avoiding the typical gaudy look of standard pet products.

## The Scope
The product scope includes:
- **EAT Collection**: Premium bowls made from hand-picked natural marble and travertine, utilizing a food-safe polish wax. Bases are crafted from warm solid wood or sleek polished metal.
- **REST Collection**: Natural beds and mats providing a perfect union of comfort and home decor. Meticulously crafted in Italy from luxurious textiles including wool, cashmere, and high-quality cotton.
- **PLAY Collection**: Toys, such as the "Topini" mice and "Pesciolini" fish, crafted from the same organic, high-quality materials as the textiles, ensuring safety, durability, and a natural fit for elegant homes.

## The Culture
Svelda values craftsmanship, sensory appeal, and organic materials. The culture is rooted in Italian craftsmanship, highlighting quality over quantity, and portraying pets as true companions deserving of fine design. The emphasis is on "living beautifully" and creating pieces that are artful additions to the home. Every stone and fabric carries its own history and character, reflecting an appreciation for the enduring beauty of the earth.
